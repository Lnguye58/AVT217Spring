• Open Adobe XD.
  • Custom Size Template Using W:20 H:20.
  • Zoom in the template for better view of the white template.
  • Click "E" on the keyboard, to be in the ellipse tool.
  • Find the middle of the template when you see a blue cross when hovering it with your mouse.
  • In ellipse tool, from the middle of template, left click and hold then drag it to the top right corner, the top and right side of the circle is touching the template.
  • Click "V" on keyboard and click outside of the white template to deselect the circle you just made.
  • Repeat step 4-6 to make 4 circles in each corner
  • Make sure the all 4 circles' color is black
  • Change border color by looking at the lower bottom right and click the rectangle box next to border to change the color
  • You're finish!
